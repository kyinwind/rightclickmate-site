诵经助手介绍

诵经助手 - 优雅的诵经体验，支持音频功能

诵经助手是一款专为佛友设计的诵经工具，提供优雅的诵经体验，支持音频功能。 主要功能包括：功课管理、诵经功能、念佛计数、拜忏功能和设置功能。 共内置40多种常见经书，供广大佛子诵经学习。

版本：1.8.1

开发者：学辉 杨

适合人群

佛友、修行者、希望通过诵经来培养专注力的人士

主要功能

  • 功课管理：提供发愿、功课管理功能，帮助佛友通过发愿建立功课计划，并记录佛友功课完成情况，督促佛友每日完成功课，精进修行。
  • 诵经功能：共内置40多种常见经书，供广大佛子诵经学习。
  • 念佛计数：提供计数器为佛友念佛、念咒计数。
  • 拜忏功能：自定义拜忏数量和忏悔文。
  • 佛学知识测试：增加佛学知识测试功能。
  • 设置功能：可以查看“功课通知设置”、“意见反馈”、“关于”、“版本”等设置功能。

常见问题

1、如何使用功课管理功能？

通过发愿建立功课计划，并记录功课完成情况， 系统会督促你每日完成功课，精进修行。

2、如何使用诵经功能？

在应用中选择你想要诵读的经书，点击开始即可进行诵经。 应用内置了40多种常见经书，供你选择。

3、如何使用念佛计数功能？

使用应用提供的计数器功能，为你的念佛、念咒进行计数。

About SongJing Assistant

SongJing Assistant - Elegant chanting experience with audio support

SongJing Assistant is a chanting tool designed specifically for Buddhists, providing an elegant chanting experience with audio support. Main features include: practice management, scripture chanting, mantra counting, repentance practice, and settings. It has built-in more than 40 common scriptures for Buddhists to learn and chant.

Version: 1.8.1

Developer: 学辉 杨

Who Is It For

Buddhists, practitioners, individuals who want to cultivate concentration through scripture chanting

Main Features

  • Practice Management: Provide vow making and practice management functions to help Buddhists establish practice plans through vows and record practice completion status, urging Buddhists to complete daily practice and advance in cultivation.
  • Scripture Chanting: Built-in more than 40 common scriptures for Buddhists to learn and chant.
  • Mantra Counting: Provide counter for Buddhists to count Buddha name and mantra recitations.
  • Repentance Practice: Customize repentance count and repentance text.
  • Buddhist Knowledge Test: Add Buddhist knowledge test functionality.
  • Settings: Can view "Practice Notification Settings", "Feedback", "About", "Version" and other setting functions.

FAQ

1. How to use the practice management function?

Establish practice plans through vows and record practice completion status, the system will urge you to complete daily practice and advance in cultivation.

2. How to use the scripture chanting function?

Select the scripture you want to chant in the app and click start to begin chanting. The app has built-in more than 40 common scriptures for you to choose from.

3. How to use the mantra counting function?

Use the counter function provided by the app to count your Buddha name and mantra recitations.
